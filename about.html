Our Story

Who We Are

At Clinical US, we are dedicated to empowering international medical graduates by providing comprehensive clinical rotation programs that bridge the gap between international medical education and the US healthcare system.

Founded on the principles of excellence, integrity, and commitment, our company is focused on helping aspiring physicians from around the world gain the essential experience and skills they need to succeed in the US medical field.

Leadership

Meet the Founder

Dr. Ann Kim

Dr. Ann Kim, an accomplished graduate of Korea University College of Medicine — one of South Korea's most prestigious medical schools — has a deep understanding of the unique challenges that international medical graduates face when transitioning to the US healthcare system.

With over five years of experience as a full-time physician at a multi-specialty practice in Lyndhurst, NJ, Dr. Kim established her own practice in Englewood Cliffs in 2014. Her passion for building strong, meaningful relationships with each patient and her commitment to listening, understanding, and caring for their unique needs are the hallmarks of her approach to medicine.

Driven by her dedication to mentoring and her commitment to excellence in medical education, Dr. Kim supports other international graduates in their pursuit of a successful medical career in the US. Her extensive experience and deep understanding of both international and US medical systems make her an invaluable mentor to those seeking to navigate this complex journey.
